Quick and Easy Ramen

This is my *recipe* for cheap tasty ramen noodles. Perfected over years of traveling and low budgets

Few ingredients needed, and little time required. Perfect for the broke and tired amoung us.

Ingredients


  • 1 package of store bought brick ramen
  • 1 & 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tabelspoon better than boulion stock of choice
  • 1 stalk of fresh green onion
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 can extra fat coconut milk
  • 1 egg

Steps


  1. In small pot bring water to boil
  2. While water is boiling prep green onion and egg
  3. Cut green onion into aprox 1mm wide rings till just the white bulb is left
  4. Add remaining onion buld to boiling water
  5. In small mixing bowl, crack one egg and beat with fork or whisk until smooth and well blended, set aside
  6. Once water is boiling, add contents of ramen flavor pack, and ramen noodles
  7. Boil on high until noodles are able to be seperated with a spoon and mixed(aprox 3mins)
  8. Once noodles are starting to get soft, add broth and all seasoning and stir
  9. Whilestill on high heat, slowly pour egg mix into pot while rapidly stirring soup. Ensure that egg gets well mixed as you pour to prevent clumping.
  10. After egg has been added and cooked for about one min, turn heat down to med-low and slowly add half can coconut milk.
  11. Stir in C-milk while on low heat until properly mixed and colors blend(do not boil or over cook C-milk as it will thin and seperate the fats)
  12. Remove from heat, pour soup into small bowl, top with green onions(optinal:add siratcha to flavor for more heat)
  13. Enjoy :)
